---

## Issues on Mobile

### 1. Column Layout
- **Problem:** Horizontal scroll feels unnatural
- **Solution:** Stack columns vertically on mobile

### 2. Touch Targets
- **Problem:** Dropdowns and buttons too small
- **Solution:** Increase padding, use larger tap targets

### 3. Text Size
- **Problem:** May be too small on narrow screens
- **Solution:** Use responsive text sizing

### 4. Drag & Drop
- **Problem:** Hard to drag on touch screens
- **Solution:** Add long-press to drag, or tap-to-move

### 5. Navigation
- **Problem:** Filters take too much space
- **Solution:** Collapsible filter bar, hamburger menu
